Mac & Cheese

Recipe for the best mac and cheese you've ever had

This recipe calls for alot of cheese and spcies in order to give this mac and cheese a bit of a kick. Everyone from young kids to heat seekers will love this recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb noodles
  • 1 lb pepper jack cheese
  • 1 lb sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 lb white chedar cheese
  • 1 lb mixed cheese
  • 1 quart heavy cream
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 3 tbs flour
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 tsp peprika
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1-2 tsp of a spcicier pepper
  • cajun seasoning 1tsp
  • green onions for garnish

    • Instructions

    1. shred cheese
    2. bring heavy cream and creame cheese to a simmer then mix in all seasoning
    3. in a seperate pan saute the green onions with the stick of butter then add flour to make a rou
    4. add rou to hevy cream spice mix then incorperate cheese
    5. after noodles are eldente add to the cheese sauce
    6. layer in pan mac and cheese, extra cheese repeat
    7. put into oven and then garnish with green onions
