Nachos

Recpie for best nachos that will knock your socks off

Nachos are o the most sacred foods, they offer both the crunch of the tortilla chip, the warmth of the melty cheese, the spice of the salsa, the coolness of the sour cream, and the elegance of the green onion garnish.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • sour cream
  • Green Onions

Instructions

  1. Gently Lay some chips on a plate
  2. Tuck them into their bed with a generous layer of cheese
  3. Heat in the microwave on high for 30 second- 1 minute
  4. Sauce it up with some picante salsa
  5. Put some sour cream into a platstic bag, cut a small hole at the bottom in order to apply sour cream ontop in a zigzag motion
  6. Garnihs with green onions
